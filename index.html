About SenseSync

SenseSync is an AI accessibility platform designed for individuals with visual, hearing, or speech impairments.

Key Features

Live Narration

Objects, text, and scenes described in real time.

Instant Captions

Speech-to-text captions and sign avatars.

Voice Generation

Natural voices generated from text or gestures.

Cross-Sensory Bridge

Connects people across different sensory needs.

How It Works

  1. Capture: Camera & microphone capture input.
  2. Understand: AI models process speech, vision, gestures.
  3. Translate: Content converted into captions, narration, or voices.
  4. Deliver: Tailored output shared in real time.

FAQ

Who can benefit?

Individuals with sensory differences, and organizations promoting inclusion.

How do I try it?

Click "Get Started" to sign up or request a demo.

What devices are supported?

SenseSync works on modern smartphones, tablets, and browsers.

Is SenseSync free to use?

Core accessibility features are free. Premium plans for enterprises are available.

Can organizations integrate SenseSync?

Yes, we offer API and SDK support for businesses and institutions.

How is user data handled?

We prioritize privacy and comply with data protection standards (GDPR, HIPAA where applicable).
